What is Drupal?

Drupal is a platform for web content management among global enterprises, governments, higher education institutions, and NGOs. Flexible and highly scalable, Drupal publishes a single web site or shares content in multiple languages across many devices.

Frequently asked questions by web editors

FAQ 1: How do I edit an existing page

This questions is very common for editors who have been signed out of the editing mode and forget to log in to edit the page.

Answer

First of all, ensure you are logged in at the Drupal 9 back end with your CDU credentials. In the same browser you are logged in, navigate to Manage > Content then search your page title. Another approach is to go to direct URL with the same browser you are logged in.
